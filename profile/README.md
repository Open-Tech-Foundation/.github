**Advocacy • Collaboration • Open Innovation**

We are a community-driven, non-profit initiative advancing open technologies — including open source, open data, open standards, and open protocols — for the public good.

---

## 🚀 Mission

Accelerate the adoption of open technologies built on essential principles:

* **Accessibility** – usable by everyone
* **Interoperability** – works across systems without lock-in
* **Security & Privacy** – safe by design
* **Transparency** – open, auditable, and trustworthy
* **Community Governance** – shaped by contributors, not control

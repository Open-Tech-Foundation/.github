## 🌐 Open Tech Foundation

**Advocacy • Collaboration • Open Innovation**

We are a non-profit, community-driven organization dedicated to advancing open technologies — including open source software, open data, open standards, and open protocols — for the benefit of everyone.

Our mission is to make essential technology accessible, transparent, and interoperable worldwide.

> Empowering people through open, standardized, and trustworthy technology.

---

## 🚀 Mission

To accelerate the adoption and development of open technologies that are:

* Accessible to all
* Vendor-neutral
* Interoperable
* Secure and privacy-respecting
* Community-governed
* Built for the public good

Open technologies promote transparency, collaboration, and innovation in digital society. ([ocf.tw][1])

---

## 🎯 Vision

A world where critical digital infrastructure is:

* Open by default
* Controlled by communities, not monopolies
* Transparent and auditable
* Inclusive and sustainable
* Free from unnecessary barriers

---

## 🧩 Focus Areas

We support initiatives across the open ecosystem, including:

### 🖥️ Open Source Software

Development, maintenance, and adoption of free and open-source software.

### 📊 Open Data

Standards and tools for accessible, reusable, and trustworthy data.

### 🌐 Open Protocols & Standards

Interoperable technologies that prevent vendor lock-in.

### 🔐 Security & Privacy

Best practices and tools for secure open systems.

### 🏛️ Digital Public Infrastructure

Open technologies for governments, education, healthcare, and society.

### 🎓 Education & Community

Training, mentorship, and awareness programs.
